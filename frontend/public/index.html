Cluster 0 : This group of customers has the lowest Purchases and Payments. Although they have sufficient funds, they tend to save rather than spend. These customers represent a segment that could be targeted with incentives to encourage higher spending.

Cluster 1 : These customers have the lowest Balance, Purchases, and Payments. With limited financial resources, they are cautious with their spending. This segment may benefit from budget-friendly products or financial assistance options to boost their purchasing potential.

Cluster 2 : This cluster includes customers with the highest Purchases and Payments. They have a good credit limit and are active spenders, utilizing their financial capacity efficiently. These customers are the most profitable segment, and retaining their loyalty should be a priority through personalized offers and rewards.
